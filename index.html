Why now

The gap is already open.

01

Students are already using AI.

Silently, without structure, without critique. Without a framework, they absorb the tool's defaults as their own.

02

Offices are hiring for it.

"AI Test-User" is now a real job title. Goldbeck GmbH is one example among a growing list across Germany. I hold the role myself.

03

Education has not caught up.

No critical frameworks, no shared vocabulary, no assessment criteria. The tool has entered the studio faster than the curriculum.

Current education produces output users. Not system directors.

Generative Design · From Prompt to Product05
Problem definition

What students cannot do
with the tools they use.

i.

Visually convincing.
Structurally weak.

Outputs look finished, but fail when scrutinised for logic, construction or use.

ii.

No control over variation.

Regeneration replaces intention. Difference without direction is not design.

iii.

No coherence across iterations.

Each image exists alone. A project is a series of disconnected guesses.

iv.

No architectural reasoning inside prompts.

Prompts describe mood. They do not encode brief, program, material or tectonic logic.

AI is used without architectural logic.

Generative Design · From Prompt to Product09
Case study · System failure + constraint

Without intent,
the system hallucinates.

Input

Empty plan.

Walls and doors only. No program, no circulation logic, no hierarchy.

Result

Spatial hallucination.

The model invents organisation. Inconsistent circulation. Functions mis-assigned. Coherence collapses.

Intervention

Architectural structuring.

Manual layout introduced: program, zones, thresholds. The system becomes functional only after architectural authorship.

AI cannot operate without architectural intent. Intent is the constraint that makes the system work.
